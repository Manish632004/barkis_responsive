Why 2 ATA Matter

Without HBOT

ATA stands for "atmospheres absolute," a pressure unit used in hyperbaric chambers.

HBOT below 2 ATA is ineffective as it doesn't significantly increase oxygen levels to produce substantial therapeutic benefits.

When breathing 100% oxygen at 2 ATA and above, physics and physiology merge as the oxygen dissolves in the blood, resulting in desired therapeutic effects— a phenomenon known as Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT).
